# Cimarron — AI Music Generation Prompts

All 17 tracks are designed as **seamless loops** (~2–3 minutes before looping) unless marked otherwise.
All prompts are **instrumental only — no vocals, no lyrics, no wordless singing or humming**.
Recommended tools: Suno AI, Udio, MusicGen, or similar generative audio platforms.
When using Suno, add `[no vocals]` and `[instrumental]` as meta-tags. On Udio, select "No Vocals" before generating.
